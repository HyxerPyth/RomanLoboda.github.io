Description

I was able to analyze bike sales of international company which have branches located in different regions of the world like Europe, North America and the Pacific. I was able to identify how average annual income, marital status, gender and degree level drives people's decisions about buying the bikes. By using the interactive dashboard that I created we can identify which group of people would be the main priority to build a targeted advertisement strategy for the marketing department. These valuable insights can inform strategic decision-making and drive business growth.
